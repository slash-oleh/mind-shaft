## TLDR template

Potential structure:

"Title" becomes "Topic" - not including any of TLDR components (Action, Condition, Reason, etc.). Just noun to recognize what it is about.

TLDR components:

```
Context
 - For: "For X"
Condition
 - When: "When possible", "Always", "Never"
 - Exception: "Unless"
Action
 - Use (What to do): "Use X"
 - Avoid (What not to do): "Avoid X", "Don't X"
Reason (to, Impact): "to X"
Examples
 - Good: "X"
 - Bad: "Y"
```

Structure:

`[Context] Condition Action [Reason] [Examples]`

Expanded:

`For A, When B, Use C. Avoid X, unless Y to Z. Good: 1, Bad: 2.`

where `[...]` - optional
